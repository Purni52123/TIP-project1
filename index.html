FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Planora?

Planora is a free travel planning and discovery app for iOS and Android. It helps you find the best places to visit, restaurants, hotels, tours and local gems across India on an interactive map, read genuine reviews, and stay connected on the go with travel eSIMs.

Is Planora free to use?

Yes. Planora is free to download and use on iOS and Android. Browsing places, reading reviews and using the map are completely free, with optional travel eSIM data plans available.

Can I list my business or place on Planora?

Yes. Business owners can add and claim their place on Planora to reach travelers, manage their listing, respond to reviews, and appear on the map and in search.

Does Planora sell travel eSIMs?

Yes. Planora includes a travel eSIM store so you can buy affordable data plans and stay connected across India without swapping physical SIM cards.

What can I find on Planora?

You can discover restaurants, cafes, hotels, attractions, tours, shopping, nature spots, entertainment and hidden local gems, complete with photos, ratings, reviews, opening hours and map directions.
